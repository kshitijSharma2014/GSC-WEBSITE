GRASSROOTS ONLINE

Date:

One month campaign(1st Dec 2013-1st Jan 2014)

About the Event:

Under grassroots online campaign,around 240 sophomores placed in different villages of India as a part of their Rural Internship were suppose to collect the data about the villages and put them online in order to bring that village online

Motive of the Event:

The main motive of the campaign was to bring the remote parts of the country online.There are villages which have no identity on the internet platform and therefore are unidentified by most of the people.The aim behind this campaignn is to make sure that this remote parts of country which are too rich in its culture,tradition and values can be easily googled up and can be identified by everyone.

INCREDIBLE INDIA PHOTOGRAPHY CONTEST

Date:

One month campaign(1st Dec 2013-1st Jan 2014)

About the Event:

This is an online photography contest where the photos that can depict any facet of India are to be published by students on Google+ page. The contest was limited to the participants of Gujarat.Primary objective was to make people aware that they can use Google+ as a platform to showcase their skills.There has been 263 entries on the page.It proved to be an online repository archiving the varied and vast culture ,flora and fauna of our nation. This event resulted as a mini- virtual museum depicting India as an incredible nation with the aid of Google+ page.
