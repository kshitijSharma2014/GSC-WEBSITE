ABOUT GSC DA-IICT

Google Students Club-DAIICT is a group of enthusiastic students who work for making the technology available to common people.The different tasks carried out by the club brings technology to the best use of common people right from teaching students about the Scratch software or bringing major events online to the campaigns launched for bringing the villages and their traditions online. Thus,Google Students' Club is an active initiative for brindging the technology and the common society.

I DECIPHER

Google Student Club and IEEE SB DA-IICT bring to you iDecipher- the ultimate test of your 'Google'ing skills! An online graphical quiz where different images as a part of a question needs to be deciphered to find the correct answer...Your only hope: Google search.


GRASSROOTS ONLINE

Google Student Club DAIICT bring to you Grassroots Online,a campaign for bringing the villages online by visiting the remote corners of the country and putting their information online in form of google maps or a blog or a youtube video or even a webpage...!!
